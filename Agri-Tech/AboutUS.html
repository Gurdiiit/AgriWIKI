ABOUT US

CoffeeNCode is young team of six passionate programmers with the mission to create web applications with the goal to better the lives of farmers all over the Indian Subcontinent. We have been working towards software solutions to make easy-to-use and interactive web applications since 19th of November, 2020. Our latest web application combines computer technologies such as Python, CSS, HTML and SQL to provide a seamless experience for farmers to find analytics, compare crop yields, receive notifications for related government policies, news and other farmers to collaborate with.
